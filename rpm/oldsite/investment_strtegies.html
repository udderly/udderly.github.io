Investment Strategies

At RPM, we take a two-pronged approach to our investment strategies: value-oriented and opportunity-driven.

As a value investor, RPM seeks to invest in properties that are selling at prices below their intrinsic value. RPM looks to take advantage of the undervalue anomalies due to markets being in distress or sellers opting to liquidate their properties for non-economic reasons.

As an opportunistic investor, RPM endeavors to adopt alternate deal structures to invest directly or indirectly in real estate properties. Structured lending with real estate collateral is an example for such strategy. Purchasing debt instruments, either performing or non-performing, is another example.
