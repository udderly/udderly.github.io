Riverview Principal Management

Riverview Principal Management, LLC (RPM) is a seasoned asset management company specializing in value-oriented and opportunity-driven investments in the real estate markets in the US.

Headquartered in the Bay area of California, RPM primarily serves institutions, family offices and high-net-worth individuals from Asia and the US.

RPM currently manages over $250 million of assets located in Northern California and Nevada. The assets under its management include multi-family residential properties and a development project.
