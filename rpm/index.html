Riverview Principal Management

Riverview Principal Management, LLC (RPM) is a seasoned asset management company specializing in value-oriented and opportunity-driven investments in the real estate markets in the US.

Headquartered in the Bay area of California, RPM primarily serves institutions, family offices and high-net-worth individuals from Asia and the US.

RPM currently manages over $250 million of assets located in Northern California and Nevada. The assets under its management include multi-family residential properties and a development project.

Third Party Managers

Pinnacle

Pinnacle is a privately held organization that manages multifamily properties nationwide. Established in 1980, Pinnacle is one of the largest multifamily management companies in the United States with a portfolio of approximately 165,000 units and 4,100 employees. Pinnacle’s clients include pension funds, private partnerships, international investors, insurance companies, lenders, special servicers, syndicators, government agencies and high net worth individuals.

ConAm

ConAm currently manages a nationwide portfolio of approximately 50,000 apartment units in more than 26 key metropolitan areas. ConAm has established regional offices throughout the US, staffed with talented management professionals who are experts in their local markets. ConAm’s regional presence and ability to respond to local conditions—combined with the resources, infrastructure and experience of a reputable national company—makes ConAm a leader in the industry.
