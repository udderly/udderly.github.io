Quadre Music Group, Inc. is a California based non-profit arts organization whose mission is to support the performance and education initiatives of Quadre. The quartet depends on donations from individuals, foundations, corporations and government agencies to support its core mission of bringing music and arts education programs to communities across the United States.

Your tax deductible donation makes it possible for Quadre to continue its mission, and for more people to forge the connection between their own life experiences and the arts. We hope you’ll consider making a donation to help keep the music playing today!

Your contribution will support:

  • National touring
  • Outreach concerts
  • Community engagement
  • International commissions
