Mission

Education

Masterclass

A Quadre masterclass offer a wealth of benefits to both performers and audience members. Through an iterative process, a small, pre-selected group of students perform in front of an audience and are given technical suggestions and interpretative feedback. By the end of the masterclass, students gain confidence and are inspired to delve deeper on their own. All attendees leave with a greater appreciation of the musical creation process.

Outreach Concerts

Committed to promoting music education in schools, Quadre excites the imaginations of kindergarteners to college students with interactive and fun programs. “Toot Your Own Horn” introduces students to classical chamber music in an accessible way. Through lively performance, story-telling, humor and instruction, Quadre engages the minds of students, who learn new critical thinking and creative skills as well as develop greater musical appreciation.

Horn Sessions

Quadre-hosted Horn Sessions galvanizes local horn communities by bringing together students from local middle and high schools in a two-day program-filled event. Students connect, bond and participate in workshops such as mock auditions, musicianship, improvisation, practicing techniques and performance. Acclaimed guest hornists lead masterclasses. In the final evening, students perform for the local community and as a grand finale, participate in what many students have called an “epic” horn choir.

Reading Days

In a small group environment, Quadre performs the works of new composers, including those by Quadre, in live reading sessions. Student hornists will be asked to give feedback and have the chance to perform alongside Quadre. A key highlight for students is the chance to discuss their interpretation of the music with actual composers. This is an exciting way for attendees to participate and engage in a musical experience.

Community Engagement

We strive to serve the communities where we live and are dedicated to engagement with the community. We recognize that this need exists throughout the nation and seek to serve every community we visit in the same manner. The purpose of our engagement is to reach out to underserved populations and the community at large. Venues for such events inclue, but are not limited to: Title IV schools, hospitals, eldercare facilities, hospices, homes for troubled youth, prisons, shopping ceners, train stations, downtown areas and sporting venues.

As our universal culture becomes more rich and diverse, we strive to make our presentations reflect the attitudes and desires of our listeners. Quadre believes that the creation and promotion of new music nurtures composers and opens the minds of young performers to new music that is essential to this cause. As we collaborate with artists of all disciplines, the synthesis of joint programs and presentations can add diversity to our own artistic expression and create new avenues to explore, which are vital to the artistic process.
