Gunn Science Alliance

Science Alliance is a group of students from Gunn High School who present science demos to third, fourth, and fifth graders. We visit elementary schools in the Palo Alto Unified School District and are passionate about science. Our goal is to excite younger children about the subject.

Started in 2008 by former Gunn student Anand Gupta, five years later the alliance continues to uphold its original mission to share the joys of science with elementary school students.
