Who we are

We are a Gunn High School club dedicated to designing, building, and flying rockets! We participate in the yearly Team America Rocket Competition, or TARC, a national competition for model rocketeers. Meetings are held every Saturday 3-5 at MakeX in Cubberly Community Center.

This year, we are building a rocket that goes to (800) ft and lands safely in one piece with (3) intact raw eggs in (42) seconds out of cardboard tubing, laser-cut wood, polyethelene foam, and a machine-controlled mechanism that releases the parachutes.
